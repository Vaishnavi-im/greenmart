Frequently Asked Questions

How long does delivery take?

We deliver within 24 hours for orders placed before 2PM. Same day delivery available in select areas.

Do you offer free delivery?

Yes! Free delivery on all orders above ₹499. A small fee of ₹49 applies for orders below that.

Are your products certified organic?

Yes, all our products are certified by NPOP (National Programme for Organic Production) India.

What is your return policy?

We offer hassle-free returns within 7 days of delivery if you are not satisfied with the product.

Do you accept bulk orders?

Yes! We supply organic products in bulk to stores, restaurants and hotels. Contact us for pricing.

How do I track my order?

After placing an order you will receive an SMS and email with your tracking link to follow your delivery.
